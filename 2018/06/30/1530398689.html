Chaos;Child - Fake Sky End




In the final release of Chaos;Child, Nono did not have a bad ending route. Said bad ending was cut from the final game but the files still exist in a broken state (the script crashes if you run it, sound effects are missing and so on). The bad end splits after Senri reveals her true form and Serika dies. Having returned to school, Senri decides to visit the grave of Nono; this time without inviting Takuru. After visiting the grave and receiving ghostly encouragement, Senri turns to head home…



I felt the presence of someone, standing behind me.

SenriMmn?

As I turned around to see their face, they too moved.

SenriHuh?

I feel a stabbing heat in my belly.

SenriUgh…

I could not grasp what had happened. As I jaggedly lowered my gaze downwards, for some reason there was something piercing my clothes and stomach, covered in red. Such an abnormal scene. This is…? Why did…? From the end of a handle, crimson stains spread across my uniform.

KawaharaIt’s your… SenriKawahara...? KawaharaEverything… is your fault.

Kawahara stands before me. With both trembling hands extended out to the handle, he watches.

KawaharaMinamisawa. It’s all your fault. SenriWhy? KawaharaKurusu has disappeared. You, who should be dead, suddenly showed up.

Kawahara stared off past me, chanting to himself.

KawaharaStrange, is it not? SenriKawahara… KawaharaIt’s you. It’s all your fault. It’s all your fault Kurusu has disappeared… KawaharaWhere are you? Give her back! Give me back my Nono…

As the puddle at my feet swells ever larger, I feel the heat of my body flow out alongside. As if a gust of wind had blown straight through the hole to cool me down.

KawaharaGive her back… Nono… Give me back my Nono!

Kawahara collapsed where he stood, gazing off into the distance whilst murmuring onwards to himself. I…

SenriUgh…

I raised my foot, as heavy as lead, and took a step forward. Forwards.

SenriI have to… get back.

Forwards.

SenriI have to get back…

Forwards.

SenriTakuru made us all tea... He is waiting for me…

Unable to stem the blood leaking from the wound, I feel my limbs grow colder still. Even so, I…

SenriI will get home.

Because.

SenriBack to them…

They are waiting for me.

SenriEveryone is waiting… for me…

Strange… The blue sky of minutes before had been engulfed in clouds. At some point, the colour had drained from this world, turning it white and black. Even so. Another step forwards. Forwards. Forwards…

SenriI have to… get… home…


YutoSis still hasn’t come home. UkiI wonder what happened. TakuruShe’s just a bit slow is all. She’s probably heading back now.

The thread of murders linked to us are all over now. Unlike before, we have nothing dangerous left to be worried about.

YutoHey, I’m really hungry. UkiNot yet. We have to wait for Senri. YutoOK.

Having said that…

TakuruSenri sure is late.

I had even gone out to buy her favourite cake especially for her.

UkiHuh? TakuruWhat’s up? UkiA siren? YutoAn ambulance? Or is it the police? UkiI’m not sure… I wonder if something happened. TakuruEither-or, doesn’t change anything.

I look up at the clock. It’s already 8.

TakuruSenri… She sure is late…


Senri did not deserve to die again.
